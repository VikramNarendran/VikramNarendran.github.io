Pioneering AI in Aviation Safety

ATRS World Conference 2026 · Beijing

Beyond the QRH: A Voice-Commanded AI Decision Support System for Pilots During Critical Emergencies

Presenting a Multi-document Agentic Decision Support System (MADSS) with PLS-SEM validation across 300+ aviation professionals. Demonstrates how domain-specific Small Language Models outperform generic LLMs in regulatory compliance and safety-critical decision making. The system retrieves QRH/FCOM/MEL procedures with AI-generated advisories, helping pilots connect related procedures and avoid common stress-induced traps during emergencies.
